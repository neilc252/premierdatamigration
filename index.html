Our Services

At Premier Data Migration, we specialize in helping businesses modernize their data, automate their IT operations, and streamline their cloud strategy. With decades of expertise in both legacy and modern systems, we deliver solutions that reduce complexity, improve efficiency, and unlock the full value of your data.

1. Data Migration & Modernization

  • Legacy Data Migration: Move data from tape archives, UNIX systems, and on-prem storage arrays into secure, scalable cloud storage (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud).
  • ERP Data Migration & Integration: Build automated pipelines that not only migrate existing ERP data but also continuously integrate new data sources.
  • Cloud-Native Data Ingestion: Design and deploy custom data pipelines to pull information from APIs, web sources, and databases into cloud data lakes and warehouses.
  • Database Migration: Transition on-prem databases (Oracle, SQL Server, MySQL, etc.) to cloud-managed platforms like AWS RDS, Azure SQL, or Google Cloud SQL.

2. IT Automation & DevOps

  • Ansible Automation: Our primary focus is leveraging Ansible Automation Platform to standardize deployments, manage configurations, and automate tasks across your entire infrastructure, both on-prem and in the cloud.
  • CI/CD Pipeline Automation: Accelerate development with fully automated build, test, and deployment workflows.
  • Storage & Data Protection Automation: Simplify provisioning, backup, and recovery processes across hybrid clouds.
  • IT Operations Automation: Automate user management, server patching, monitoring, and incident response to reduce manual overhead.

3. Cloud & Systems Consulting

  • Cloud Strategy & Architecture: Design secure, scalable cloud environments (AWS, Azure, GCP, OCI) tailored to your business needs.
  • Legacy System Assessment: Evaluate UNIX servers and legacy apps, then create clear modernization roadmaps.
  • Efficiency Audits: Identify opportunities for automation, cost savings, and performance improvements across your IT landscape.

4. AI & Emerging Tech Integration

  • Web Scraping & Data Extraction: Extract, process, and structure data from public websites and digital sources, delivering it in a clean, usable format for your business needs.
  • AI/ML Integration: We help you integrate AI and Machine Learning models into your data pipelines and business processes to unlock new insights and drive innovation.
  • Custom Scripting & API Integration: Solve unique integration and data processing challenges with Python, Bash, Perl, and more.
  • Data Pipeline Orchestration: Ensure reliable data flow, transformation, and delivery through robust, automated pipelines.
